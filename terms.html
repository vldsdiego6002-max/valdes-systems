Terms of Service

The plain version: I build automation systems for your business. You pay me. If either of us isn't happy, we can walk away. Here are the details.

These terms are between you (the business owner using my services) and me, Diego Valdes, doing business as Valdes Systems out of Saint Marys, Georgia.

What I provide

I build and maintain automation systems for home-service and trades businesses. The exact services and pricing depend on what we agree to on our call — whether that's missed-call text-back, lead follow-up, review automation, a website, or any combination from my pricing page.

How payment works

  • A one-time setup fee is due before I start building
  • Monthly retainers are billed on the same day each month after that
  • All payments go through Stripe (industry-standard secure payment processor)

Cancellation

What I'm responsible for

  • Being honest about what the system can and can't do for your business

What I'm NOT responsible for

  • Outages caused by your phone carrier or third-party tools beyond my control

Liability

If something goes wrong because of me, my maximum liability is whatever you've paid me in the last 3 months. That's standard for service businesses and protects us both from outsized claims over relatively small engagements.
