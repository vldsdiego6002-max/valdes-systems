Privacy

// LAST UPDATED · MAY 2026

Short version: I don't sell your data. I don't share it with marketers. I use what you give me to do my job — that's it.

The live demo: nothing you type into the demo on the home page is saved, recorded, or sent anywhere. The whole demo runs on your phone or computer's screen — your name and number never leave your device.

What I collect

When you book a call through Cal.com, that platform collects your name and email so it can send the calendar invite.

If you become a client, I'll also have your business contact info, billing details, and the configuration of the system I build for you. That's it.

What I do with it

  • I use your name and email to call you back if you booked a call
  • I use your business info to build, run, and maintain your system if you sign up
  • I do NOT sell your contact info, share it with marketing partners, or add you to any third-party newsletter list

What I don't see

If you become a client and I set up your missed-call text-back:

  • I never see or store your call content
  • I never read your customers' messages
  • The system only reads ring/answer status to trigger the text-back
  • Your customers' conversations stay between you and them

Third-party tools

I use a few tools to run this business. They each have their own privacy policies:

  • Cal.com for booking calls
  • Stripe for payments if you become a client
  • Cloudflare to host this website

Want me to delete your data?

Email me at diego@valdessystems.com and ask. I'll delete what I have within 7 days and confirm when it's done.

Questions?
